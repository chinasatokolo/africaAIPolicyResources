# Africa AI and Digital Policy Resources
A curated list of resources for those conducting research on AI policy in Africa. List created by [Chinasa T. Okolo, Ph.D.](http://www.chinasatokolo.com), a Computer Science PhD graduate from Cornell, UN AI policy specialist, WBG-Africa Fellow, and former fellow at the Brookings Institution, the world's leading think tank.
